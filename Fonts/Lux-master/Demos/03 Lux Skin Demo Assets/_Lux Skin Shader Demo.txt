LUX SKIN SHADER DEMO ASSETS

You may import the „Head_Level2.OBJ“, create a new material and select the Lux Skin Shader.
Assign the related textures like this:
- „Head_Diff.jpg“ —> Base(RGB) Alpha(A)
- „Head_Normal.jpg“ —> Normal Map
- „Head_SpecGlossSSSAO.psd“ —> Specular(R) Roughness(G) SSS(B) AO(A)
You should check „byPass“ sRGB Sampling for this texture.

Finally assign the new material to the head object ;-)
